Digital*U

Mobile Design Contest

September 4th - December 19th

* What is it?

Coinciding with the roll-out of the m.ubc.ca mobile platform, Digital*U is a campus-wide contest for ideas for mobile apps and services that would dramatically enhance the UBC experience.

* Prizes

$5000
First place

$3000
Second place

...plus cool devices and a spot on the front page of UBC.ca. Winners will be based on votes submitted by the campus population, and decided by a panel composed of both students and mobile developers
