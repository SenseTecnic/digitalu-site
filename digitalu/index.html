Digital*U

Mobile Design Contest

October 15th - January 15th

* What is it?

Help us invent UBC's digital future! Win great prizes by describing a new mobile app or service that could dramatically enhance the UBC experience! It can be for fun, function or achieving UBC's global mission. We're looking for creative individuals and groups on campus to champion innovative ideas.

The Digital*U competition will run from October 15th, 2012 to January 15th, 2012 . The best ideas will find their way into labs, design studios, hackathons and -- yes -- into reality!

* Prizes

$5000
First place

$3000
Second place

...plus cool devices and campus fame & fortune to be won for sharing your cool ideas for a connected campus.

* How to enter

Full contest details will be released on October 1st, 2012. Meanwhile, start getting your ideas and teams together!

Submissions will be judged based on originality, feasibility, potential impact and benefit to UBC, and will be judged on the following components:

  • a stand-up presentation to a panel of judges
  • a tangible demonstration of your idea (i.e. a detailed story or prototype)
  • a 90-second promotional video
  • a 1,000 word written submission

Let's invent the future here. Your dream can change our world!
